How to play:

In this game, the goal is to match all of the cards. Click a card to turn it over, and then click whichever card you think will match it. If they're not compatible, they'll flip back over so you can try again. Have fun!

Rules

1. Click on a card in order to flip it over.
2. Then click on another card to flip it over.
3. If the cards are a match, they stay flipped over, but if they aren't a match, the cards are flipped back over.
4. Flip over all of the matching pairs to win the game.
